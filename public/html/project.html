      Inspiration

      I felt inspired by several different things. In no particular order:

      Abandoned Exploration

      I enjoy watching videos and seeing photography of abandoned places around the world. There is a somber, lonely feeling to them.

      Source: Seph Lawless

      The Backrooms

      The horrific idea of the Backrooms, which started as a 4chan post, really inspired me. The feeling of being in a place so familiar yet unfamiliar is so terrifying to myself. I think what makes the Backrooms so unnerving, is the feeling of 'emptiness' and the discomfort of being in a completely empty place.

      Source: Wikipedia

      Being Lost in a Mall

      Being lost in a shopping center as a child is a very stressful experience, and I wanted to capture this stressful feeling within my game.

      Exploratory Horror Games (Silent Hill Series)

      I have played the Silent Hill games using Emulator or on the Playstation 2-3 as a child, and I loved that the games were open to partially-open world, yet filled with horror- which was unusual in the 2000-2010s, as many horror games were in enclosed, controlled environments with a more linear progression system.

      Silent Hill Bêbado Voltando Pra Casa GIFfrom Silent Hill Bêbado Voltando Pra Casa GIFs

      Source: Tenor

      Plot Summary

      Act 1 - Daytime

      You are exploring a mall with the aim to find your mother, however, if you take a detour, some malicious things may begin to happen.

      Act 2 - Nighttime

      During the night, the player can choose to hide overnight in the Mall, but you will have to avoid Security Guards and, if necessary, some benevolent entities...

      Act 3 - The Ritual

      The final act of this game is finding out the dark history of this Mall, and trying to vanquish the evil within it...
