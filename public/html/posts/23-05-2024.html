The Mall Devlog

Mock up of Eggscellent!

23-05-2024

Finally beginning to develop again after the very stressful exam season, I spent a total of ~4hrs developing 3d models and placing them within the store to get an idea of what I want the final store to look like. I want it to be a warm, inviting place where you can happily sit down and eat and relax with your friends.

I also created a new shader for the store front sign that makes use of the previously created window shader, where instead, I added a option to add an image as an underlay.

TOOLS:
- Blender 3d
- Unity
